Legend

Manhole — sewer access point. Note depth, surface type (flagstone, soil, etc.), and any landmarks.
Cleanout — household sewer cleanout. Note unit number and surface covering.
Root intrusion / repair — known or suspected root damage, prior repairs, areas needing excavation.
General note — anything else worth flagging (valves, meters, hazards).

How to add a marker

  1. Click one of the colored buttons above (Manhole, Cleanout, Root/Repair, or General Note).
  2. Click the spot on the blueprint where that feature is located. A pin will appear and a form will pop up.
  3. Fill in a short label (e.g. "MH-2 near B2/B4", "Unit A7 cleanout") and any details — depth, surface covering, access notes, photos you've taken, etc.
  4. Click Save. The pin stays on the map and appears in the list to the right.
  5. To edit or delete a pin later, click it (in Browse mode) and use the form that pops up.
  6. Use the zoom buttons (top right of the toolbar) or pinch/scroll to zoom in on dense areas before placing pins precisely.

Sharing & saving your work

Markers you add here are saved to the shared map automatically — everyone who opens this link sees the same pins. The export/import buttons below are just for keeping your own backup copy or printing a paper version for a plumbing crew.
